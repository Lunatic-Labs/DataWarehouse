.Types of Development
****
Green Field:: 
* New 
* Freedom
* Uncharted Territory
* High 
** *High* Risk
** *$$$* - innovative, more expensive
** *more* fun

Brown Field::
* Existing Work/standards
* Constraints
* Well Understood (by the industry)
* Low 
** *Low* Risk
** *$* lower risk, less innovation, less expensive. 
** *less* fun
****
